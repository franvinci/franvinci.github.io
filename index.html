About me

I'm Francesco, a 25 y.o. Ph. D. Student in “Brain, Mind and Computer Science” at the University of Padua. My research focuses on process mining, where I apply Machine Learning and Data Science techniques to analyze and optimize organizational processes.

I have a strong passion for math and an enthusiast interest in exploring the intersection of technology and human cognition.

I'm based in Padua but originally from Messina. I'm dedicated to advancing knowledge in my field and making valuable contributions to the academic community.
